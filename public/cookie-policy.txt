## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. Cookies help make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

Cookies can be **session cookies** (deleted when you close your browser) or **persistent cookies** (saved until they expire or are deleted). They may also be **first-party** (set by us) or **third-party** (set by services we use, such as analytics providers).
